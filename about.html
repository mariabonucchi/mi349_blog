About the Author: Maria Bonucchi

Maria Bonucchi is a rising senior at Michigan State University earning a Bachelor of Arts in Public Relations, with a double minor in Information and Communication Technology and Development, and Leadership of Organizations. Along with holding off campus jobs, Maria is also the President of the Wharton Center Student Marketing Organization where she organizes events and media campaigns for the Wharton Center.

She is currently the Global Services: Communications Intern at Tenneco where she works with the communications and HR teams on corporate connections. In the upcomoing school year, Maria will serve as the Public Relations Intern at the Wharton Center supporting the Marketing and Communications Department on all Broadway and performing arts shows.
